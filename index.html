About me

All my life I have been surrounded by health and fitness, it is something I have a true passion in. From my early teens I was a keen runner, I then discovered the gym and all that comes with it. I started lifting weights but found I wasn’t getting very far. However, research into nutrition and proper workout routines I started seeing result. it was at this time I found my passion, nothing could stop me, I decide to go to university and study sport and exercise therapy. The human anatomy is extraordinary, I had great pleasure in learning about how the body works. Whilst studying, I chose to challenge myself and enter multiple bodybuilding competitions, seeing how nutrition and fitness can change the body fascinates me.
I became a personal trainer as I truly believe I can help, I believe there is no fitness goal too big. I am driven, motivated and determined to making fitness dreams a reality. why? because nothing brings me more pleasure than seeing someone achieve something, they thought was impossible.
